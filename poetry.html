ON THE PAVEMENT

I found him dead.
The blue sky, he tried to reach
Master of the sky, who used to be?
Flight far, flight high
He was the master of the sky.

Up there, the home he thought
Flight, run, he never looked down
The ground, from where his world begins
Stumble, falling, he soared back up
To where he thought he belongs
To where his eyes were the world
Many wonders, many places, he saw it all
With pride, he faced the world.

But today, he feels tired
His wings are hurt, his heart is sored
Stumble down, he lets it free
No longer master
The blue sky doesn't hold him back
Away, away, far, far, he is falling down.
And there he sees
The ground, from where his world begins
He is coming back, but not falling down
He closes his eyes, he smiles to the world
He is coming "home", at last he realises.

I found him dead.
His face is to the dirt
The wide sky, he didn't try to reach
The ground, he wants to explore
But no time he has it left
The falling tears
I see on his face,
and over my cheek
I bury him in a shallow grave of dirt,
in the embrace of home,
in the nostalgia of live
I turn away
I sweep my face
And I run
And I fly.

LIKE A WATER HYACINTH

Like a water hyacinth, as the flow of time
I move onwards
Left behind the life I had
The bonds broken into pieces
The puzzle of live, crack and scratch
Never be a whole
Gather, I try to gather, time after time
A sand castle I always build.

The lesson I have learnt
Like a water hyacinth
Floating on forwards
Never could I turn
I let the time shatters me away
Where would I go, who am I become, when will I stop
I let the time shatters me away.

And when time, it comes
Thunder, storm, the strongests join for celebration
Pick up myself, I join for celebration
And here I am, prisoned in pages
The one place I feel safe
The one place I feel home.
Tomorrow comes a new life
An empty page, awaiting me to be trapped.
They say it's book
I say it's my life
Open, close, finish, unfinish, nobody cares
I learned my lesson
I let the time shatters me away.

One day, time's dead, and so freedom I'll have
Like a bird sets free to the sky
Wide, open, I fly
The chapter I will finish
The book I will close
The puzzle I will burn
A water hyacinth, as dust I will become.
